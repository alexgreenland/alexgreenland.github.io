Montréal

Vibrant, hockey-centric, creative

The largest city in Québec and second largest in Canada, Montréal boasts a rich and diverse culture. Simultaneously remembering the past and looking to the future, the metropolis is a leading centre of finance, technology and the creative industries. The whole city unites when the Habs are playing.

Alex Greenland
